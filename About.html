Athletic Scholarships

An athletic scholarship is a form of scholarship to attend a college or university or a private high school awarded to an individual based predominantly on their ability to play in a sport.The NCAA awards more than $2.9 billion in athletics scholarships annually.48% of students receive federal scholarships.

Merit Scholarships

A merit-based scholarship is an award given to high performing students. It is based entirely on your merit in either academics, sports, leadership activity, community service or other special interests rather than your need for financial aid.There are two kinds of rank given to a person. One is common merit rank and other is category rank.

Economical Scholarships

The EWS Scholarship Program is a part of the PM Young Achievers Scholarship Award Program, a scholarship program run by the Central Department of Social Justice and Empowerment.Under this program, students from economically disadvantaged areas are eligible for scholarships.
